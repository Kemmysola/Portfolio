What Do I Want?

  • Chisom O. Fredrick
  • Content Writing
  • 14

I want to be successful, that tomorrow you see me and know all these days weren't days of luck. I only want to put my heart and pain in what I love. Showing so much determination in things to prove.

I don't know where I am going yet but I got to search cos I hope. I know I'm meant for something greater and with time we'll all get to see the change like weather.

What do I want, you ask again?

Someone who sees the light in me like no one else, someone to stand by me and watch me clean up my wounds and heal up the scars, who's so sure of me and never second guesses, someone who listens to all my messes and weirdness and still feels soothed.

Who gets mad sometimes yet never so much to end what we've framed...who makes me splufic in everyway.

What do I want?

I want to see everyone I love smile, want to know their tears are that of joy, see them happy genuinely and for a very long time, not some slacky wins. Let's hurdle so many records, keeping the team tighter and stronger. Just as pushing with the same aim and keeping a close lane.

I Guess That's All For Now.
